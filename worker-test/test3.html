Bitmovin Player Test

The objective of this test is to collect Sample Data from Video playback with Bitmovin Player.

To complete this Task please go to the following Page and complete these Tasks:

  1. Start Playback of the Video
  2. Watch the Video for about 20 Seconds
  3. Jump to another location in the Video
  4. Watch another 20 Seconds
  5. Press Pause
  6. Rate your satisfaction with the Playback of the Video
